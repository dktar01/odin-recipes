Lasagna

Thanks for taking the time to look up this simple Lasagna recipe! I will be providing you with ingredients, prep time, and cook time so you can enjoy this too!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Your favorite pasta sauce
  • Your choice of ground beef or italian sausage
  • Cottage Cheese
  • 1tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • basil

That is all the ingredients required for this recipe, below I will give you the prep list!

Prep

  1. Fill a pot with 4 cups water and set stove top on high, adding a generous amount of salt to the water.
  2. Place noodles in pot of water when water is boiling
  3. Prepare sauce in a seperate pot, place burner on low
  4. Brown your choice of meat in another pan, adding spices and garlic
  5. Once meat is browned, add to sauce in pot and bring to simmer
