Chicken Parmesan

Here is a recipe for Chicken Parmesan, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • flour
  • eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Your favorite red sauce, or make from scratch
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic
  • Butter

Prep

  1. Hammer or flatten chicken
  2. whisk eggs in a container to cover flattened chicken
  3. cover in flour
  4. heat a pan with olive oil and a tbsp of butter
  5. fry chicken for 4 1/2 minutes per side or until golden brown
  6. cover in sauce and paremesan cheese
  7. Enjoy!
